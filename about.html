About League of Legends

What is League of Legends?

League of Legends is a team-based strategy game where two teams of five champions face off to destroy the opponent's base. Choose from over 140 champions to make epic plays and secure victory.

Game Roles

  • ADC (Attack Damage Carry): Ranged damage dealers
  • Support: Utility and protection
  • Mid Lane: Versatile champions
  • Jungle: Roams between lanes
  • Top Lane: Tanky or bruiser champions

Champion Types

  1. Assassins – High mobility, high damage
  2. Fighters – Balanced offense and defense
  3. Mages – Magical damage dealers
  4. Marksmen – Ranged physical damage
  5. Support – Utility and protection
  6. Tanks – High health and defense
